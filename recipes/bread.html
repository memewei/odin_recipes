Pain au Chocolat

Description

Pain au chocolat is a type of Viennoiserie pastry consisting of a cuboid-shaped piece of yeast-leavened laminated dough, similar in texture to a puff pastry, with one or two pieces of dark chocolate in the center.

Usually sold or served warm from the oven, they are commonly sold alongside croissants in French bakeries and supermarkets.

Ingredients

  • 500g puff pastry block
  • 100g dark chocolate, broken into small pieces
  • 1 egg, lightly beaten
  • Icing sugar, for dusting
